Saylani Welfare

Saylani Welfare is on the ground and already working with local communities to assess how best to support underprivileged families in more than 63 areas of day to day lives.

Saylani Welfare Services

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-profit organization based in Karachi, Pakistan. It was founded by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui in 1999 with the aim of providing various welfare services to the underprivileged segments of society. The organization operates a wide range of services including.

Education

Saylani Welfare International Trust runs schools and educational programs for children who cannot afford formal education. They also provide vocational training to equip individuals with skills for employment opportunities.

Healthcare

Saylani operates medical clinics and hospitals that provide free or subsidized healthcare services to those in need. This includes medical consultations, diagnostic tests, and treatment.

Food Distribution

One of the prominent services provided by Saylani is the distribution of free meals to the needy. They run soup kitchens and food distribution programs to alleviate hunger in impoverished communities.

Disaster Relief

Saylani Welfare International Trust also engages in disaster relief efforts, providing aid and assistance to communities affected by natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and droughts.

Marriage Support

The organization supports financially struggling families by arranging and sponsoring marriages for deserving individuals

Orphan Support

Saylani provides support and care for orphaned children, including education, healthcare, and other essential needs.

Water Projects

Saylani has initiated projects to provide clean drinking water to communities that lack access to safe water sources.

Skill Developmen

Saylani offers various skill development programs and vocational training courses to empower individuals with the skills needed to earn a livelihood.
